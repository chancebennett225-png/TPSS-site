Meet Your Program Director

Chance A. Bennett

Hello, and a warm, sparkling welcome! I'm absolutely thrilled to share the heart and soul behind **The PinK School of STEM**.

My goal is to spearhead and direct an **all-girls STEM program**, built on the belief in the significant impact proper STEM exposure imposes on a young academic experience. As a single mother of one, I'm committed to providing this education to young girls like my own daughter, inspired by witnessing firsthand the potential often lost due to a lack of resources and exposure.

My Background & Vision

My vision is to impact Education beyond the classroom by mastering and innovating methods of **Curriculum Design**. I aim to develop a **universal lesson method** applicable to all subjects and learners.

My academic foundation is an extensive background in **Modern Biology**, including numerous research projects. This passion for Science, specifically Biology, is coupled with my creative foundation in **Fashion Design and Fashion Drawing**, which sharpened my capacity to engage and execute from a creative perspective.

My teaching extends to designing learning activities, providing mentorship, and utilizing data for **learner assessment** to enhance outcomes. My dedication to student-centered learning and **creative problem-solving** has been instrumental in my success providing informal teaching and curriculum development.

Despite early academic setbacks, I persevered through challenges like homelessness and unemployment. My strong faith and determination led to exceptional grades later in my journey, reflecting the importance of commitment and faith in God. I am also experienced in community outreach and service-learning, notably with the **Boys & Girls' Club of America**.
