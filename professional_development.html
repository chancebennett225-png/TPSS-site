Educator PD: Integrating AI & Biology in STEM            
               

Professional Development Strategy

           

Integrating AI-Powered Learning with Biology for Middle School Girls            

           
               

Target Audience: 6th-8th Grade Teachers & Tech Instructors

               

Program Context: Pink School of STEM Summer Program | Duration: 120 Minutes

           
       
               

1 Framework & Strategy

We ensure all educators are grounded in our core philosophy: the critical intersection of AI, Biology, and Gender-Responsive Teaching for middle school girls.

  • Welcome, Objectives & PD Context
  • Gender-Responsive STEM Strategies
  • Activity: Quick Think-Pair-Share on comfort and concerns with AI tools.
Key Takeaway: Alignment with AI, biology, and gender-responsive teaching is non-negotiable for modern STEM education.

2 AI Tools in Biology

Educators learn to make abstract concepts tangible using cutting-edge tools to enhance learning and personalized paths.

Recommended Tools:

  • AI-powered Virtual Labs & Interactive Simulations
  • Molecular Modeling & Genomics Visualization Platforms

Core Strategy:

Using AI for personalized learning paths, interactive assessments, and collaborative project development.

Key Takeaway: AI tools make abstract biology concepts tangible and highly engaging.

3 Digital Citizenship

We embed ethical reasoning directly into the science. Staff teach students to become responsible tech users through critical analysis.

Core Components:

  • Ethical AI Usage & Data Privacy
  • Source Verification & Digital Footprint Awareness
  • Activity: Scenario Challenge (Genetic privacy, data collection ethics)
Key Takeaway: Embedding digital citizenship creates responsible tech users in the next generation.

4 Assessment & Rigor

AI is used to enhance academic rigor by aligning digital projects and assessments with Louisiana State Standards.

Techniques Covered:

  • AI-powered Formative Assessments
  • Digital Project-Based Learning (PBL) Reports
  • Using AI for concept review and interactive study guides.

Activity:

Teachers create a standards-aligned biology assessment incorporating an AI tool.

Key Takeaway: AI enhances both learning and assessment while maintaining academic rigor.

5 Support & Success

Successful implementation requires strategic planning and ongoing support. We ensure staff leave with a clear action plan and resource network.

Success Metrics:

  • Teacher confidence levels & Student engagement rates.
  • Assessment performance & Digital literacy growth.

Ongoing Support:

  • Monthly Check-in Meetings
  • Online Resource Repository & Peer Mentoring System
Key Takeaway: Successful implementation requires strategic planning and ongoing support systems.
       

Special Considerations for Excellence

We ensure Differentiated Support for all tech comfort levels and maintain a strong focus on engaging traditionally underrepresented groups in STEM, emphasizing practical, immediately applicable strategies in every session.

This PD session is part of a recurring series, continually building expertise in AI-enhanced biology experiences.
