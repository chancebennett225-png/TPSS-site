About The Pink School of STEM

Our program is an innovative, non-traditional curriculum constructed by skilled STEM educators. We merge AI Literacy with fundamental Biology concepts to elevate the learning curve for middle school girls.

Our Core Principles

Curriculum-Informed AI (CI-AI)

We ensure all AI tools are instructionally aware and aligned with Louisiana Science Standards, embedded within proven teaching strategies.

Interdisciplinary Reasoning

Activities are designed to encourage students to transfer concepts from AI to explain phenomena in science, fostering deep understanding.

Project-Based Learning (PBL)

Our thematic framework avoids expository teaching, emphasizing active learning through real-world problem-solving.

AI Ethics & Digital Literacy

Students learn about bias detection, algorithmic awareness, and responsible use of AI, gaining more than just technical skills.

Our Integrated STEM Curriculum

Our narrative-driven curriculum transforms learning into a mission. Explore the unique challenges and AI integrations for each grade level.

6th Grade: The Structure of Matter

This module explores the fundamental building blocks of the universe. Students investigate the structure of matter and the properties of waves, analyzing them as patterns in physical science.

Science Focus

Structure & Properties of Matter (atoms, molecules); Wave Properties (refraction, reflection).

The Challenge

Analyze patterns of cosmic matter and communication waves to identify the "source code" of new life forms.

AI Integration

Use Google's Teachable Machine to train image classification models, learning about Machine Learning, data input, and algorithmic bias.

7th Grade: The Flow of Energy & Matter

Students track the flow of energy and matter through complex systems. This module connects life science concepts like photosynthesis with Earth science phenomena like atmospheric circulation.

Science Focus

Cycling of Matter & Flow of Energy (Ecosystems); Atmospheric Circulation & Climate.

The Challenge

Predict how disturbances in an ecosystem's energy flow, like in Louisiana wetlands, are recorded by atmospheric patterns.

AI Integration

Develop computational models and use virtual labs. Practice prompt engineering with Generative AI chatbots as personalized tutors.

8th Grade: Engineering & Environmental Solutions

The final mission is to apply problem-solving skills to real-world environmental challenges. Students engage in the engineering design process to create solutions for issues facing Louisiana.

Science Focus

Engineering Design (ETS1); Natural Hazards & Human Impact (Louisiana fossil fuels/coastal issues).

The Challenge

Design a sustainable, AI-monitored system to mitigate human impact on Louisiana's resources or coastal environment.

AI Integration

Use AI to analyze large environmental data sets. Use Generative AI to design and critique solutions, evaluating the validity and reliability of AI-generated claims.

THE FOUNDATION: HOLISTIC PROGRAM DESIGN

🗓️ 8-WEEK PROGRAM STRUCTURE

The curriculum is structured as a three-act plot, **The Eternal Code**, fostering **Project-Based Learning (PBL)** and **AI Literacy**.

  • I. ACT I (Wks 1-3): **The Scattering:** Mastering Classification, Data, and Modeling using AI (Grade 6 Focus).
  • II. ACT II (Wks 4-6): **The Flow Crisis:** Mastering Computational Thinking and Adaptation via Simulations (Grade 7 Focus).
  • III. ACT III (Wks 7-8): **The Eternal Design:** Mastering AI Critique, Design, and Optimization (Grade 8 Capstone Focus).

👩‍🏫 EDUCATOR & EQUITY FOCUS

Program success is driven by comprehensive **Professional Development (PD)** centered on best practices in inclusion and technology.

  • 📘 **Culturally Responsive Teaching (CRT):** Training ensures curriculum relevance and belonging for all students.
  • ♿ **Universal Design for Learning (UDL):** Implementing course materials in diverse formats (text, audio, visual) for accessibility.
  • 💻 **AI Tool Mastery:** PD focuses on **AI-powered virtual labs**, ethical use, and integrating tools like **Teachable Machine** and **GenAI Chatbots**.

THE MISSIONS: 8 WEEKS OF ACTION

ACT I: THE SCATTERING (6th Grade Focus - Wks 1-3)

Mastering Classification, Data, and Modeling using AI

🧬 Wk 1: DNA & Machine Learning

Define AI/ML/ANN. Use Scratch 3.0 to manipulate simple algorithms, preparing for complex AI concepts.

🔦 Wk 2: Wave Properties & TM

Hands-on Light Lab. Use Teachable Machine (TM) to train an image classification model to differentiate wave patterns.

🔬 Wk 3: AI Ethics & Cells

Project: The Unbiased Cell Classifier. Evaluate TM model for algorithmic bias by ensuring data variety in cell inputs.

ACT II: THE FLOW CRISIS (7th Grade Focus - Wks 4-6)

Mastering Computational Thinking and Adaptation via Simulations

🧠 Wk 4: Energy & Neural Networks

Activity: The Neuro-Synaptic Bridge. Compare human neuron structure to the Artificial Neural Network (ANN) structure for transfer learning.

🌊 Wk 5: Ecosystem Flow Crisis

Project: The Wetland Flow Crisis. Use Virtual Labs to model matter cycling and energy flow, simulating pollutant impacts.

🧬 Wk 6: Adaptation & GenAI

Project: Adaptive Evolution Case File. Use Generative AI Chatbot as a tutor to help construct an explanation of genetic adaptation.

ACT III: THE ETERNAL DESIGN (8th Grade Capstone - Wks 7-8)

Mastering AI Critique, Design, and Optimization

🔥 Wk 7: Thermal Defense Engineering
  • 🛠️ **Design & Test:** Design, construct, and test a device to minimize or maximize thermal energy transfer.
  • ✍️ **AI Task:** Use **GenAI** to generate design ideas and then **critically evaluate and propose refinements**.
⚖️ Wk 8: Final Capstone & AI Ethics
  • 🏗️ **Final Project:** Design a sustainable, AI-monitored system (The Pink Sentinel) for coastal erosion mitigation.
  • 🎤 **Pitch & Ethics:** Use the **Magna Amata Matrix** for ethical analysis and present a final pitch, communicating findings and evaluating AI data validity.

GIRL-FRIENDLY PEDAGOGY

Our curriculum uses **high-leverage strategies** to foster **confidence, resilience, and belonging** in STEM.

Heavy emphasis on group work and **"exploratory talk"** to share ideas and **"talk themselves into understanding"** complex concepts.

Cultivating a **"growth mindset"** by rewarding strategy and resilience to persist through challenges.

🖐️

Semantic Wave Instruction

Managing cognitive load by oscillating between **abstract AI concepts** and concrete, **hands-on science activities**.

The Mother of Science

Magna Amata represents the complete **Eternal Genome**—the ultimate blueprint of evolution and scientific knowledge. She is not a single mentor, but the unified, perfect state that her five daughters (Seraphina, Electra, etc.) strive to attain.

In our narrative, Magna Amata was the entity whose **DNA was scattered** by a cosmic event, leading to the creation of the five sisters, each embodying one piece of her vast knowledge (Cellular Energy, Nervous System, etc.). Her story frames the entire curriculum, showing students the power of **unity, knowledge integration, and cosmic responsibility**.

Role in the Program:

Magna Amata serves as the **Vision and End Goal**. Students follow the "Protocols" derived from her saga to master the scientific standards necessary to restore the Eternal Genome.

The Magna Amata Saga: Synopsis

The epic narrative centers on **Magna Amata**, the original genetic template of complex life, whose cosmic form was shattered and scattered across five dimensional planes by the malevolent **Silver Triplets**.

Drs. Rachel and Roman Rice detect the genetic fragments on Earth, synthesize them, and unknowingly give birth to five daughters (Seraphina, Electra, Prismatica, Radiance, and Fuchsia Flame). The story follows the girls' supernatural growth and their subsequent merging back into a unified **Magna Amata** to fight the Triplets.

The saga culminates in a war against entropy, where the daughters orchestrate a planetary defense system powered by biological evolution and the cosmic force of love.

Meet Seraphina: The Power of Cellular Energy

Seraphina, the embodiment of pure energy, is here to show you how the smallest biological processes fuel the greatest forces in the universe. Her strength comes directly from understanding **Cellular Respiration**.

With her ability to summon and control flames, she dynamically represents how every living thing generates and uses power at the cellular level. She is the raw, unstoppable force that fuels all biological systems.

Louisiana Science Standards Alignment

  • **Grade 7: LS1.C** (Organization for Matter and Energy Flow in Organisms)
  • **Grade 7: PS3.D** (Energy in Chemical Processes and Everyday Life)
  • **Science Practice:** Developing and Using Models (Modeling the transfer of energy)

Key Scientific Concepts

🔥

Mitochondrial Power

Seraphina’s powers mirror the **Mitochondria**, the 'powerhouse' of the cell that converts nutrients into ATP (energy).

⚡

The ATP Cycle

She teaches **Energy Transfer**, showing how organisms utilize ATP to instantly fuel any action, from thought to movement.

💡

The Laws of Thermodynamics

She illustrates how energy is conserved and transferred, never created or destroyed, in biological systems (**Bioenergetics**).

Seraphina's Visual Diary

Meet Electra Blush: The Nervous System

Electra Blush, with her ability to sense and manipulate bioelectricity, is a living metaphor for the **nervous system**. She represents the lightning-fast communication that allows us to think, move, and feel.

**The Science Behind Her Powers:** Electra’s powers are a dynamic representation of **Bioelectricity** and **Neural Networks**. Our bodies communicate using electrical signals transmitted through neurons. She embodies this complex network, showing how a single spark becomes a chain reaction of information.

Louisiana Science Standards Alignment

  • **Grade 6: PS4.A** (Wave Properties, Transmission, and Absorption - relating electrical signals)
  • **Grade 8: LS1.D** (Information Processing - focusing on sensory reception and nerve impulse)
  • **Science Practice:** Developing and Using Models (Modeling the neural pathway of response)

Key Scientific Concepts

⚡

Bioelectricity & Ion Flow

She teaches the function of **voltage-gated ion channels** and how sodium/potassium pump drives nerve impulse propagation.

🧠

Neural Communication

Electra demonstrates the speed and efficiency of **synaptic transmission** and how neurons form information processing networks.

👁️

Sensory Information Processing

She explores how the nervous system interprets external stimuli and transforms energy signals (like light or sound) into perceptions.

Electra's Visual Diary

Meet Prismatica: Optics and Photobiology

Prismatica, the master of light and spectrum, demonstrates how we perceive the world. Her powers are a dazzling representation of **optics** and how biological systems utilize **electromagnetic radiation**.

She embodies **Photosynthesis** and **Wave Behavior**. Just as a prism splits light, Prismatica helps us break down complex visual information, showing how light energy is captured and transformed.

Louisiana Science Standards Alignment

  • **Grade 6: PS4.B** (Electromagnetic Radiation - focusing on visible light and color)
  • **Grade 7: LS1.C** (Organization for Matter and Energy Flow in Organisms - specifically Photosynthesis)
  • **Science Practice:** Constructing Explanations (Explaining how light energy is transformed into chemical energy)

Key Scientific Concepts

🔮

Wave Behavior (Optics)

Explaining how light **reflects** (like a mirror) and **refracts** (like a lens) to form images.

🍃

Photobiology (Photosynthesis)

The process where plants convert light energy into chemical energy (food) using chlorophyll.

🌌

Electromagnetic Spectrum

She details the full range of light energy, explaining how different wavelengths (colors) affect matter.

Prismatica's Visual Diary

Meet Radiance: Ecosystems and Energy Flow

Radiance, with her ability to heal and transfer energy, is a profound representation of **ecosystems** and the flow of energy through them. She is a reminder that all living things are connected and depend on each other for survival.

**The Science Behind Her Powers:** Radiance’s powers are a metaphor for **Food Webs, Energy Pyramids, and Conservation**. Just as energy moves from the sun to producers and then to consumers, Radiance transfers life-giving energy. She shows us that balance is key to a healthy system and that a single positive action can have a ripple effect.

Louisiana Science Standards Alignment

  • **Grade 7: LS2.B** (Cycle of Matter and Energy Transfer in Ecosystems)
  • **Grade 7: LS2.C** (Ecosystem Dynamics, Functioning, and Resilience)
  • **Science Practice:** Analyzing and Interpreting Data (Evaluating stability and change in energy systems)

Key Scientific Concepts

⬆️

Trophic Energy Transfer

She teaches how energy is gained and lost as it moves from **Producers** (plants) up to **Consumers** (animals) in the food web.

🔄

Biogeochemical Cycles

Radiance models the endless cycling of vital matter, like **Water** and **Carbon**, demonstrating the resilience of nature.

🛡️

Ecosystem Resilience

She explores how **Biodiversity** keeps systems healthy and how human impact affects the long-term survival and healing (resilience) of ecosystems.

Radiance's Visual Diary

Meet Fuchsia Flame: Genetics and Evolution

Fuchsia Flame, with her ability to change and adapt her form, is a powerful symbol of **genetics** and **evolution**. She represents the incredible potential for change encoded in our DNA.

**The Science Behind Her Powers:** Fuchsia’s powers are a metaphor for **Genetic Variation and Natural Selection**. She embodies how small changes in our genetic code can lead to incredible adaptations over time. She reminds us that our potential for growth and change is written into our very DNA, making variation a source of strength.

Louisiana Science Standards Alignment

  • **Grade 8: LS3.A** (Inheritance of Traits - focusing on genes and DNA)
  • **Grade 8: LS4.B** (Natural Selection and Adaptations)
  • **Science Practice:** Engaging in Argument from Evidence (Arguing for how mutations provide evolutionary advantages)

Key Scientific Concepts

🧬

Genetic Code & Variation

She teaches the mechanisms of **Inheritance** (Mendelian and complex traits) and how genetic variety ensures a species' survival.

🦊

Natural Selection & Adaptation

Fuchsia demonstrates how **Mutations** (random changes in DNA) provide the raw material for adaptation and evolution over time.

🏭

Gene Expression (Proteins)

She covers how DNA is read to make proteins, linking the genetic code to the physical traits (phenotypes) students observe.
